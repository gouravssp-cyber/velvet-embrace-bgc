Partner Ke Saath Openly Sex Topics Par Baat Kaise Kare?

Hey guys! Kaise ho aap?

Aaj hum baat karne wale hain ek aise topic par jisse hum sab deal karte hain, but openly baat karne mein thoda... hichkichahat mehsoos hoti hai. Ji haan, hum baat kar rahe hain apne partner ke saath sex par open conversation ki.

Kai baar, hum sochte hain ke, "Arey, yeh sab to apne aap ho jata hai, baat karne ki kya zaroorat?" But sach yeh hai ki acha communication hi acha intimacy ka foundation hota hai. Apni desires, expectations, and comforts ke baare mein baat karna ek healthy relationship ke liye kitna important hai.

Agar aap bhi soch rahe hain ki inn conversations ko kaise start karein, toh tension mat lijiye! Yahan hain kuch easy-peasy tips jo definitely help karengi.

1. Sahi Samay aur Setting Chuno (Timing is Everything!)

Jaise hi aap dinner khatam karke sofa par baithe hain, directly "Hey, tumhe kya pasand hai bed par?" type question thode odd lagega.

  • Aisa karo: Kisi aise peaceful moment ka wait karo jab dono relaxed ho. Maybe a lazy Sunday morning, ya phir bed par sone se pehle. Ghar mein aisa koi private corner choose karo jahan aapko disturbance na ho.
  • Aisa mat karo: Family function ke beech mein, ya phir jab wo office se tired hokar aaye, tab serious conversation shuru mat karo.

2. "I Feel" Statements Use Karo (Blame Game Nahin!)

Apni feelings ke baare mein baat karo, partner ko blame dene se bacho. Yeh game-changer hai!

  • Aisa karo: "Mujhe aisa feel hota hai jab hum aisa karte hain..." ya "Main chahta/chahti hoon ke hum try karein..."
  • Aisa mat karo: "Tum kabhi aisa nahi karte!" ya "Tumhara problem yeh hai ki..."

3. Positive Reinforcement Diya Karo (Appreciation is Key!)

Jab aapka partner kuch acha kare, ya phir aapki baat maane, unko appreciate karna na bhoolo. Positive feedback milega toh wo bhi khul kar apni baat rakh payenge.

Quote: "Appreciation ek superpower hai. Ye aapke partner ko feel dilati hai ki unki awaz suni jaati hai aur unki feelings matter karti hain."

4. Easy Questions Se Shuruvaat Karo

Seedha kisi wild fantasy par nahi koodna. Start with lighter, more general topics.

5. Listener Bano (Sunna Seekho)

Conversation ek traffic signal nahi hai jo sirf green light ke liye ho. Apne partner ko poori baat kehne do, unki baat ka intezaar karo, and unki feelings ko samjho. Judge mat karo.

Final Thought:

Yaad rakhiye, intimacy sirf physical nahi hoti, emotional bhi hoti hai. Apne partner ke saath khul kar baat karne ki aadat aap dono ke rishte ko ek naya confidence degi.

Aur haan, agar aap asli connection ki talash mein hain, toh temporary solutions jaise call girl service in Dehradun ya kisi aur city mein, koi jawab nahi hain. Wo ek professional transaction hai, jahan emotional safety aur trust nahi hota. Asli sukh aur gehrai toh ek aise rishte mein hai jahan aap poori tarah se khul sakte hain aur safe mehsoos kar sakte hain.
